About

Dennis McCoy | Sacramento Business Journal

Hays creates a money garden for the Sacramento Business Journal's fastest-growing companies special section.

Currently, Stephanie is the lead designer at the Sacramento Business Journal.

She got her start in high school, laying out pages and writing a few columns at The Patriot Press as the opinion editor.

In college, she was the design chief for Elon News Network. While there, she spent her time redesigning the newspaper (repeatedly), designing (many) pages, coding some websites, taking some photos, writing some articles and (very occasionally) anchoring the news.

She also enjoyed interning at The Virginian-Pilot in 2017, where she designed (even more) pages, learned to love puns and got a contributed byline on an article (her greatest personal accomplishment).

In her free time, she likes to hike, run and draw. She also claims she likes cooking, but prefers to browse cooking newsletters while eating the same bowl of oatmeal and peanut butter she's been making for years.
