About

Dennis McCoy | Sacramento Business Journal

Hays shows off stamps she designed for the Sacramento Business Journal's Women Who Mean Business special section.

I’m currently a designer at The Seattle Times, where I layout local news, features and entertainment sections.

Previously, I was the lead designer at the Sacramento Business Journal where I oversaw the design of our weekly paper, brainstormed big ideas for special sections and fiddled with code to improve our web design.

My favorite work is always a team effort and usually involves trying something out of the ordinary, whether that’s building Lego sculptures for special sections or collaborating on a last-minute cover story about Sacramento’s new MLS team.

Previously, I’ve worked at The Virginian-Pilot, where I designed daily news and sports pages. I also led the design team for three years at Elon News Network, where I redesigned our print paper, launched our special projects site and helped create the inaugural Best Of special section.

When not thinking about news design, you can find me taking photos, going for a run or checking out hiking trails.
