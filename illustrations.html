Sacramento Business Journal

Fastest Growing Companies

Page design and photo illustration | When thinking of ways to show the growth of companies, plants were a recurring theme. So I decided to unite how we measure company growth (in money) with a typical picture of growth (plants). Hence, money plants

Sacramento Business Journal

Sacramento Business Journal's 2018 Best Places to Work

Page design and photo illustration | To create the idea that open communication leads to happy employees, I cutout speech bubbles and professionals and laid them on colorful paper. The illustration was photographed, printed, copied and then scanned back onto the computer to give it an old-school office look.

The Virginian-Pilot

Piling Up

Page design and illustration | This article was about baseball players responding (or not responding) to fan mail. I created an illustration, wrote a corresponding headline and added baseball stamps and a stamp drop cap to link everything together.

First place from the Michigan State University Society for News Design for sports page design

Sacramento Business Journal

The innovation awards

Page design and illustration | This page was the start of a cover story about the winners of SBJ's Innovation Awards, so I played with an "idea explosion" theme.

Sacramento Business Journal

No vacancy

Page design and photo illustration | To represent the idea of a lack of wet lab space in the Sacramento region, I juxtaposed typical lab equipment with the bright neon glow effect used on old motel "no vacancy" signs. The headline "No vacancy" was used on the cover, while the inside features a pull quote as the secondary headline.

Elon News Network

After polarizing election, Elon works toward unity, compassion

Page design and photo illustration | After the 2016 election, Elon students were nervous and angry. To show the importance of being able to reconcile and have both sides work together, I drew figures with red and blue accents to show the coming together of people from different political ideologies.

Sacramento Business Journal

The rise and resurgence of Sacramento's banks

Page design and illustration | To show how many banks were coming back to the Sacramento region, I decided to literally build the word "banks."

Page design and illustration | One of the overarching themes of this panel was on telemedicine and telehealth. The combination of typical medical care and patient devices like phones and computers was used as the inspiration for the illustration.

Elon News Network

The war on mold

Page design and illustration | There had been complaints of mold in Elon residence halls, but there were no photos. So I decided to make the newspaper look as though it were being torn open as a result of mold.

The Virginian-Pilot

Shattered image

Page design and photo illustration | After an academic scandal, I wanted to show how a well-known sports school had been damaged. Since a trophy is a typical sports icon, a shattered one conveyed the point.

Sacramento Business Journal

CFO of the year

Page design and illustration | I drew inspiration for this special section from design details on the one dollar bill and incorporated them throughout, and made a complete fake CFO bill for the centerspread.

Elon News Network

Unbalanced

Page design and illustration | One of ENN's reporters investigated how much Elon's president made in comparison to the rest of the faculty. To show the imbalance, I illustrated a seesaw, weighed down at one end with stacks of money and a jibjab of our president.

Elon News Network

For the culture

Page design and photo illustration | In honor of Black History Month, I wanted the front page of the BHM special section to incorporate important moments and people who have been key at Elon in increasing and maintaining diversity.

Elon News Network

Shielded by screens

Page design and photo illustration | Since fake personas and overly posed photos are common on Instagram, emojis plastered over the faces of a real photo replicated the insincerity that can be present on social media.
